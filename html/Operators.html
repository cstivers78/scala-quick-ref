Operators

Precedence

In order of highest to lowest.

  1. all other special characters
  2. * / %
  3. + -
  4. :
  5. = !
  6. < >
  7. &
  8. ^
  9. |
  10. all letters: [a-zA-Z]

See Scala Reference.

Declaration

  • X unary_~ – ~ is a unary operator → ~x
  • X :~(x: X) – ~ is left associative → x :~ y == x.:~(y)
  • X ~:(x: X) – ~ is right associative &arr; y ~: x == x.~:(y)
